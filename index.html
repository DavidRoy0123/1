What is the Bible?

The Bible is a collection of sacred texts revered by Christians worldwide. It is divided into two main sections: the Old Testament and the New Testament, comprising 66 books written by various authors inspired by God.

It serves as a guide for faith, offering wisdom, history, poetry, prophecy, and teachings that have shaped civilizations for centuries.
